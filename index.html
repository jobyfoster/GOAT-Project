Jeff Bezos

Who is he?

Jeff Bezos is the founder and former CEO of Amazon, the world's leading e-commerce platform, which he started from his garage in 1994 and grew into a global powerhouse. Besides his business ventures, Bezos is known for his interest in space exploration, exemplified by his aerospace manufacturer and sub-orbital spaceflight services company, Blue Origin.

What does he do now?

Jeff Bezos owns Blue Origin, a company focused on commercial space travel, and he still holds significant shares in Amazon even after stepping down as CEO. Currently, he's engaged in various philanthropic efforts, such as the Bezos Day One Fund, and continues to pursue his long-term vision for space exploration and other technological innovations

Why's he the GOAT?

Jeff Bezos is the GOAT because of his impact on the world of e-commerce and technology. His vision and leadership in creating Amazon, along with his ambitious pursuits in space exploration with Blue Origin, have marked him as one of the most influential and innovative figures in modern business history. His ability to disrupt traditional industries and continually push the boundaries of what's possible has led many to view him as a true game-changer, deserving of the GOAT label.
